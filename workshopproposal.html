Workshop Overview + Aim

Strength education, clarity, and relief from musculoskeletal pain, and live fascia-related demonstration in one accessible session.

Designed especially for adults above 40, while remaining relevant for any participant who wants better mobility, posture, strength, energy, and practical daily wellness habits.

  • Presentation and discussion led by Coach Simran Agarwal and Dr Anuraag Jaiswal.
  • Live fascia manipulation or pain/posture demonstration on 1-2 selected volunteers where appropriate.
  • Clear real-life guidance instead of gym intimidation, crash diets, or one-size-fits-all fitness advice.

Aim: help participants connect stiffness, fatigue, weakness, or pain with practical next steps in strength, nutrition, posture, and fascia care.

Camp Proposal

The workshop can extend into a 1-day or 2-day wellness camp when 30-40 participants want deeper practical support.

1-day camp

  • Screening for pain, stiffness, movement limitation, and common postural concerns.
  • Selective fascia manipulation support for localized pain or restriction.
  • Guided movement, mobility, and practical exercise coaching.
  • Basic strength and healthy ageing routines participants can continue afterward.

2-day camp

  • Greater scope for individual fascia-related intervention and participant handling.
  • More structured live workout sessions and corrective movement work.
  • Deeper guidance on pain awareness, recovery, exercise progression, and sustainable habits.
  • Better fit for larger or more engaged audiences wanting practical take-home support.

Camp audience: clubs, schools, institutions, residential communities, private groups, business forums, and wellness-oriented organizations.

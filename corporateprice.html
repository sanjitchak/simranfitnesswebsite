HR Positioning Script

We do not run a generic fitness program. We start with a physiotherapist-led movement health assessment. First, we screen employee posture, workplace sitting patterns, functional movement, and ergonomic risk. Then we classify employees into low, moderate, and high-risk groups. From there, we run a structured corporate wellness program focused on postural correction, movement efficiency, stress-movement education, and workplace injury prevention. The company gets a clear HR summary, participation data, risk insights, and recommendations for the next step. This is not gym training. This is preventive workplace movement health.

Internal rule: Do not price this like a simple wellness workshop. The scientific edge is the postural screening, ergonomic audit, risk stratification, and HR reporting.

Verbal Pricing Script

We usually work in three formats. The first is a one-time Energy & Strength Audit workshop, which is good for a wellness day or pilot. The second is our 30-day reset, where employees go through a structured corporate wellness program with tracking and a final HR summary. The third is a 90-day transformation program for companies that want deeper behavior change. For most companies, I recommend starting with the free pilot workshop. If your team likes it, the 30-day program usually starts from around ₹1.25 lakh depending on team size and number of batches.

Internal rule: If you price the 30-day program below ₹50,000, it will look like a local trainer service. The sweet spot is ₹1 lakh to ₹2 lakh for a department-level corporate program.

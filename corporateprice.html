Pricing Quiz

Question 1 of 6

Which package are you quoting?

Choose only one level.

Level 2: Group Training Program 12 sessions per month for employees. Main monthly revenue offer.
Level 1: Discovery Workshop 60 to 90 minute lead generation workshop. Free for first 1 to 2 companies, then paid.
Level 3: Executive 1-on-1 Master Program Personal program for executives, founders, senior leaders, and frequent travelers.
Question 2 of 6

What format will you deliver?

Offline has higher pricing because of travel, customization, and in-person correction.

Offline Best for posture assessment, group correction, and corporate premium pricing.
Online Best for remote teams and lower-cost continuation.
Hybrid Offline assessment plus online follow-up.
Question 3 of 6

How many participants?

For Level 3, enter 1 unless you are quoting multiple executives.

Question 4 of 6

What session plan?

Group training is fixed at 12 sessions per month. Executive program can be 8, 12, or premium.

12 sessions/month Default group plan. Also used for executive 3x/week plan.
8 sessions/month Executive 2x/week plan.
Premium access + 12 sessions Executive premium plan with WhatsApp/call support and travel planning.
60 to 90 minute workshop Use for Discovery Workshop only.
Question 5 of 6

What commitment are they choosing?

Use continuation discount only for multi-month commitment.

1 month / one-time No continuation discount.
3 months 5% continuation discount.
6 months 10% continuation discount.
12 months 15% continuation discount.

Any premium additions?

Keep this simple. Do not overload the quote.

Reference Pricing for Kolkata Corporate Companies

Use this as the internal pricing guide. The generator uses these ranges.

Package Recommended Kolkata Corporate Pricing Notes
Level 1: Discovery Workshop Free for first 1 to 2 companies. Then ₹15,000 to ₹45,000. Premium corporate or 75+ employees: ₹45,000 to ₹75,000. 60 to 90 minutes. Fitness, nutrition, posture, stress, and sleep awareness.
Level 2: Offline Group Training ₹5,000 to ₹7,500 per employee/month. Minimum company billing ₹60,000/month. Sweet spot: ₹75,000 to ₹1,50,000/month for 10 to 20 employees. 12 sessions/month. Includes posture assessment, group workouts, nutrition guidance, stress and sleep coaching.
Level 2: Online Group Training ₹2,500 to ₹4,500 per employee/month. Minimum company billing ₹35,000/month. Sweet spot: ₹45,000 to ₹1,00,000/month for up to 30 employees. Good for remote teams and continuation plans.
Level 3: Executive 1-on-1, 8 sessions ₹24,000 to ₹35,000/month per executive. 2 sessions/week. Best starter executive plan.
Level 3: Executive 1-on-1, 12 sessions ₹36,000 to ₹50,000/month per executive. 3 sessions/week. Strongest regular executive plan.
Level 3: Premium Executive ₹60,000 to ₹90,000/month per executive. 12 sessions plus WhatsApp/call access, travel nutrition planning, and real-time problem solving.
Monthly Retainer Discounts 3 months: 5% off. 6 months: 10% off. 12 months: 15% off. Do not discount the first workshop. Discount only continuation.
Do not underprice Level 2 below ₹60,000/month offline. If you go too low, HR will see it as a local fitness class, not a corporate wellness program.

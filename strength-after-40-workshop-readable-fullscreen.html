Slide 11 — Start Here

What To Start With After 40

Frequency

Train 2–3 times a week on non-consecutive days.

  • Start with two full-body sessions.
  • Keep at least 48 hours between hard days.
Weekly rhythm

Movements

Squat, hinge, push, pull, carry and core stability.

  • Pick one move from each pattern.
  • Use machines, bands or weights as needed.
Six patterns

Volume

1–3 sets of controlled reps with gradual overload.

  • Stop 1–3 reps before failure.
  • Add load only after reps feel steady.
Build slowly

Priority

Form, joint comfort and consistency over heavy lifting.

  • No sharp pain, bouncing or rushing.
  • Leave each session feeling capable.
Form first
Simple rule: choose movements you can repeat without pain.
Progress rule: add reps, range, control or load slowly.
Say Think squats, lunges, rows, chest press, lat pulldown and basic carries, scaled to the person in front of you.
